VPN

A Virtual Private Network encrypts your data and keeps your activity private and out of the wrong hands. A VPN service gives you online privacy and anonymity by creating a private network from a public internet connection. Do you need access to corporate applications and resources to other branch offices with no regional restrictions? Benefit from the functionality, security and management of the private network. (We give you the best and quality personal virtual private network infrastructure that helps protect your privacy and have access to media content with no regional restrictions. Strong encryption and no-log policy.)

Data Center

A Data Center is a facility that centralizes an organization’s shared IT operations and equipment for the purposes of storing, processing, and disseminating data and applications. Since IT Operations are crucial for business continuity, a data center is vital to the daily operations of businesses. (With our Data Center, your organization's core information system (IS) is always protected.)

Hosting

Hosted services are applications, IT infrastructure components or functions that an organization access from external service providers through an internet connection. Hosting services are most often used for hosting Web sites but can also be used for hosting files, images, games and similar content. Web hosting is a service that allows organizations and individuals to post a website or webpage onto the internet. (Ozone provides reliable, robust and secure hosting services with full management tools.)

Wan

A Wide Area Network is a type of computer network that spans a relatively large geographical. A WAN links multiple sites together and allows communication among these sites either on different sides of state, regions or countries. For a business with several branch offices, a WAN service is vital as it makes it possible to centralize critical operations.

Mpls

MPLS is the acronym for Multiprotocol Labelling Switching. It is a routing technique that directs data from one node to the next based on short part labels rather than long network addresses. It is a simple, secure telecommunications solution because you do not have to deal with encryption or special hardware. MPLS technology and applications are excellent ways to bolster your security, grow and connect everyone you need to, wherever they are.
